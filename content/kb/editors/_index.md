List of things that I have been liking / disliking about different editors.

- nvim
  - :green_circle: Extremely configurable.
  - :green_circle: Extremely fast.
  - :green_circle: Huge ecosystem.
  - :red_circle: Limits of TUI
    - Not being able move with keeping cursor out of the view.
  - :red_circle: Not single binary.
  - :red_circle: Spending too much time on configuration.
- zed
  - :green_circle: Best OOTB experience for coding.
  - :green_circle: Fast.
  - :green_circle: Single binary.
  - :red_circle: No characteristics of its own.
  - :red_circle: Not extremely customizable.
  - :red_circle: Multi buffer is not for everyone.
- helix
  - :green_circle: Single binary.
  - :red_circle: Limits of TUI
